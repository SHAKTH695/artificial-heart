Jarvik 7 Artificial Heart

About the Artificial Heart

The Jarvik 7 artificial heart model is designed to demonstrate the components and working principle of an artificial heart. This model is made using accessible materials to showcase the structure and function of the artificial ventricles.

How the Model Was Made

The model consists of various components such as artificial ventricles, inflow and outflow grafts, pneumatic driver, and valves. The ventricles are made using lightweight materials, and the connecting tubes represent the blood flow pathways.

Model Components

  • Artificial Ventricles (Right & Left)
  • Inflow and Outflow Grafts
  • Valves
  • Pneumatic Driver
  • Exit Cannula
